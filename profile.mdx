## Upgrade membership

Click **Upgrade** in the **Profile** or **Pricing** in the top navigation bar to view the complete pricing plans and compare benefits. By subscribing to a paid plan, you unlock premium features such as **Source Code Export**, **Custom Domain**, the latest models, and priority access to new features.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-34.png)
</Frame>

**Plan recommendation**: We strongly recommend prioritizing the **Pro** plan to experience the platform's full capabilities.

<Note>
  **Note**: Monthly credits are only valid during the active membership period. Once the membership expires, any unused monthly credits will be cleared to zero.
</Note>

## Account management

Click **Account** to manage your personal identity information and account security.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-29.png)
</Frame>

- **Edit profile**: Modify your username here. To ensure account security, the email address bound during registration cannot be modified directly within the app.
- **Change password**: Regularly update your password to protect your account. Changing the password requires email verification.
- **Delete account**: Permanently remove your account and data if you no longer use AutoCoder.cc.

<Warning>
  **Warning**: Account deletion **takes effect immediately** and is **irreversible**. Once confirmed, all your data (including generated projects, remaining credits, and account history) is permanently cleared and cannot be recovered. Active paid subscriptions are also canceled immediately. **Proceed with caution.**
</Warning>

## Billing management

Click **Manage Billing** to securely redirect to the official our global payment partners billing portal. Here, you can independently complete the following operations:

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-32.jpeg)
</Frame>

- **Manage payment methods**: Add, remove, or update your payment channels. Currently supported methods include Secure Payment Gateway Link fast checkout, as well as major credit cards like Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and UnionPay.
- **Update billing information**: Modify the email address used to receive electronic invoices and billing notifications.
- **View billing history**: Check historical deduction details and download electronic invoices.

## Feedback

If you encounter system bugs, functional anomalies, or have suggestions for improvement, click **Feedback**:

- Select the corresponding **Feedback Type** in the pop-up form, describe your issue in detail (up to 1000 characters), and click **Submit** to send it directly to the product team.
- For in-depth customer support or business collaboration inquiries, email the official support team directly at: `support@autocoder.cc`.

Click **Log Out** to securely end your current session and return to the preview interface.
