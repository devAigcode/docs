## What you'll learn

- **Define and optimize your requirements** — shape a clear brief before generation starts
- **Generate a structured demo in minutes** — go from prompt to working preview
- **Customize pages, content, and design** — refine the output to match your vision
- **Manage your site through a simple backend** — edit without touching code
- **Deploy and share your project instantly** — publish with one click
- **Download and extend your code for advanced use** — own the codebase when you're ready
