### See. Edit. Build.

Building software with AI often feels like a black box.

You enter a prompt. You wait.

And several minutes later, a complete project appears.

The problem is that if the AI misunderstood your idea, you only discover it after the entire build is finished.

[AutoCoder's](https://www.autocoder.cc/platform?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=latest&utm_campaign=ProgressiveBuild) new Progressive Build experience changes that.

**Instead of jumping directly from prompt to full project, AutoCoder now builds your project in three visible and editable stages.**

---

## Stage 1: Editable Draft (~2 min)

The first stage generates an editable draft focused on:

- UI style
- Layout structure
- Visual direction

Unlike a traditional preview, this draft is already part of your project.

As soon as it appears, you can:

- modify layouts
- adjust styles
- refine content
- continue chatting with AI

Your project becomes editable within minutes, not after completion.

## Stage 2: Requirements (~5 min)

Once the draft is ready, AutoCoder generates a structured requirement list.

This stage defines:

- pages
- user flows
- features
- business logic
- system structure

Instead of discovering issues after generation, you can review the blueprint before the full build begins.

This gives you more visibility and control over the final result.

## Stage 3: Full Project (~15 min)

After requirements are confirmed, AutoCoder builds the complete production-ready system.

This may include:

- Web applications
- Mobile apps
- Admin management systems
- Customer portals
- Staff dashboards
- Multi-role workflows
- Backend APIs
- Database infrastructure

Rather than generating isolated pages, AutoCoder assembles the complete software ecosystem required by your business.

Most importantly, any edits made during the Draft stage are automatically synchronized into the final project.

Every refinement becomes part of the finished product.
