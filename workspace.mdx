## Personal project management

In the **In Progress** tab of your **Workspace**, you can centrally manage all your project drafts and deployed applications. You can perform the following actions on a single project:

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-37.png)
</Frame>

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Edit" icon="pen">
    Click **Edit** to open the AutoCoder.cc editor. You can continue modifying your project's code or UI using natural language.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Preview" icon="eye">
    Click **Preview** to view a full-screen interface. Use the quick access button on the right to instantly jump back into edit mode.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Remix" icon="clone">
    Select **Remix** from the `...` menu to clone the project. This creates a new draft for safe testing or derivative development.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Delete" icon="trash">
    Select **Delete** from the `...` menu to permanently remove the project. _(Note: Ensure the project is unpublished or backed up first)._
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Published project management

Manage publication settings and status for online projects in the **Published** tab of the **Workspace** (or the **Publish** pop-up within the editor):

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-38.png)
</Frame>

### Domain settings

Manage your project's access address in the **Domain Setup** module:

- **Subdomain**: View or modify the system-assigned `.autocoder.cc` suffix domain.
- **Custom Domain**: Bind a domain you have purchased on a third-party platform to the current project. This requires configuring a CNAME and uploading an SSL certificate.

### Project sharing and public visibility

Toggle the **Share to SparkVibe** switch to control whether the project is publicly displayed in the **SparkVibe** community. Once enabled, other users can **Like** or **Remix** your work.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-39.png)
</Frame>

### External link redirection

After successful deployment, the system provides a fixed **Website URL** (frontend link) and **Backend URL** (management backend link). Anyone can directly access your web application through these public links without an AutoCoder.cc account.

### Unpublish

To take the project offline, click the **AutoCoder.cc Subdomain** button at the top of the interface to interrupt external access.

## Liked project management

### Likes tab

Projects you **Like** in the **SparkVibe** community are automatically saved here.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-40.png)
</Frame>

**One-click reuse**: View your inspiration library at any time and **Remix** a project directly. Convert it into a new draft in your **In Progress** tab to continue developing based on the original author's work.
