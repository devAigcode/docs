## Why starting from a Google Maps link matters

Most local businesses already have a presence on Google Maps: a name, an address, photos, opening hours, and a set of customer reviews built up over years. That information is the hardest part of a website to write from scratch, and it is already public. Starting from the Maps link means the site is built on what the business actually is, rather than on placeholder copy you have to replace later.

From there, the work that usually requires a designer and a developer — laying out services, pricing, portfolio, and staff profiles, wiring up online booking, and building an admin dashboard to manage it all — is generated for you and stays editable. Even without coding or web design experience, you can go from a Maps link to a complete, working website for a nail salon, hair salon, spa, or other local business.
