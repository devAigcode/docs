## Project launch

Launch your project externally, including web deployment and domain configuration.

### Web deployment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Publish">
    Click **Publish** in the top right corner of the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Web tab">
    Select the **Web** tab in the pop-up window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your domain">
    Configure your domain (**Subdomain** or **Custom Domain**). Optionally, toggle **Share to SparkVibe** to share your project with the community.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm publication">
    Click **Publish** to confirm your deployment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-23.png)
</Frame>

After deployment, the system generates two links: the frontend display link and the **Admin Dashboard** link for managing full-stack data.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-42.png)
</Frame>

### Subdomain

All users (including Free plan users) receive an `*.autocoder.cc` **Subdomain**. Enter your desired prefix in the **Subdomain** configuration tab to automatically assign it.

### Custom Domain

<Note>
  This feature is exclusive to paid plans (the **Standard** plan supports 1 domain, and the **Pro** plan supports unlimited domains).
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your domain">
    Enter your third-party domain in the **Custom Domain** tab of the **Publish** pop-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a CNAME record">
    Log in to your third-party domain provider (e.g., AWS, GoDaddy) and add a CNAME record pointing to the target address provided by AutoCoder.cc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify DNS">
    Return to AutoCoder.cc and click **Verify** to check the DNS resolution status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload SSL certificate">
    Manually upload your SSL certificate files (`.key` and `.crt`/`.cer` formats) to enable HTTPS.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## App launch

Complete app packaging and test distribution.

<Note>
  The **App** tab is only displayed when your project includes a mobile application.
</Note>

### App store launch

*Currently, the platform does not support directly publishing the generated app to Google Play or the Apple App Store. You must handle compliance and the submission process manually.*

### Test package download (Android / iOS)

If you generated a mobile app, switch to the **App** tab after clicking **Publish**.

*Currently, AutoCoder.cc only supports generating and downloading Android APK test packages for installation testing. iOS test packages (.ipa files) and TestFlight distribution are not supported at this time.*

### Source code export

- **Source Code Export** is a core feature of the paid plans (**Standard** and **Pro**). The Free plan does not support this operation.
- Package and download the complete production-grade code generated by AutoCoder.cc (including frontend UI, backend logic, and database schema) to your local machine. Deploy it to your own cloud server or a third-party hosting platform to achieve 100% ownership of your digital assets.
