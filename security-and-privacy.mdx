## Account Authentication

**Account Authentication** is used to ensure that only authorized users can access and manage their own projects and data.

### Login & Registration

You can use the full features of the platform by registering and logging into your AutoCoder.cc account, such as:

- Create projects
- Edit Demo
- Remix community projects
- Manage Workspace projects

After logging in, your projects, task progress, and account information will be automatically saved to your account.

### Google One Tap Login

To simplify the login process, AutoCoder.cc supports **Google One Tap Login**. When logging in with Google, you can:

- Click "Sign in with Google"
- Select your Google account
- Quickly complete the login

This method allows you to quickly enter AutoCoder.cc and start creating applications without needing to create an additional password.

- How AutoCoder.cc collects user information
