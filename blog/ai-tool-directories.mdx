### Step 1: Prepare Your Assets

Before you start submitting, prepare these assets:

- Clear product description (usually under 300 words)
- High-quality logo (square format recommended)
- Screenshots or demo video
- Pricing information (free, freemium, or paid)
- Categories that best describe your product

---

### Step 2: Prioritize Your Submissions

Not all directories are equal. A recommended order:

1. Start with **large comprehensive directories**
2. Submit to **niche directories related to your category**
3. Plan your **Product Hunt launch strategically**
4. Follow up with **smaller directories for additional backlinks**

---

### Step 3: Track Your Submissions

Create a simple spreadsheet tracking:

- Directory name
- Submission date
- Status (pending / approved / live)
- Listing URL
- Traffic or leads generated

This helps you identify **which directories are actually driving results**.

## FAQs
### Are AI tool directories free to submit to?

Most AI tool directories, including [MossAI](https://mossai.org?ref=autocoder), offer free submissions. Some platforms provide premium featured listings, but free listings are usually sufficient for gaining initial visibility.

### How long until I see results?

Traffic can start arriving within a few days after your listing is approved. However, SEO benefits from backlinks typically take **4–8 weeks** to become noticeable in search rankings.

### Should I submit to multiple directories?

Yes. Different users prefer different directories. Submitting your product to multiple AI directories increases visibility and helps diversify your traffic sources.

### Can I submit if my tool is still in beta?

Most directories accept beta products. Just make sure to clearly state that your product is in beta and explain what users can expect.
