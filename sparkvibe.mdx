**SparkVibe** helps you discover outstanding projects in the AutoCoder.cc community, share your own work, and quickly create new projects through **Remix**. Gain inspiration, understand different project logic, and build products faster through community showcases and interactions.

In the SparkVibe module, you can **browse** community projects, **Like** and **Remix** projects, or publish your own projects to the community for others to use upon approval.

---

## Preview

View projects created by other users and directly experience the demo using the **Preview** feature.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-10.png)
</Frame>

In **Preview** mode, you can:

- **View** page effects.
- **Switch** between different pages.
- **Understand** the overall project structure.

---

## Remix

If a project's structure or design fits your needs, **Remix** it directly.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-11.png)
</Frame>

**Remix** copies the project's structure and design, creating a new draft in your **Workspace** under the **In Progress** tab. This allows you to modify and expand the project, accelerating your creation process without building from scratch.

---

## Like

Click **Like** to save projects that inspire you.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-12.png)
</Frame>

Liked projects are saved to your **Likes** tab, making it convenient to quickly view or reference them later.

## Search & sorting

To help you quickly find projects of interest, SparkVibe provides search and sorting features.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-13.png)
</Frame>

### Sorting

Sort projects to quickly discover trending applications in the community:

- **Popular**: Most popular projects.
- **Latest**: Most recently **Published** projects.
- **Likes**: Projects with the most likes.
- **Remix**: Most remixed projects.

### Keyword search

Use the keyword search box to quickly find specific projects. Enter keywords related to project names, industries, page formats, or features to locate targets efficiently.

Combine category tags and sorting methods to further narrow your search.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-14.png)
</Frame>

**Examples:**

- Enter `Restaurant` to find food and beverage projects.
- Enter `Landing Page` to view landing page examples.
- Enter `Mobile App` to find mobile application projects.
