## What you'll learn

- **Stage 1 — Editable Draft (~2 min)** — generate the visual design and layout, then refine UI, content, and structure in real time before completion
- **Stage 2 — Requirements (~5 min)** — review pages, features, and workflows and validate business logic before generation to reduce costly rebuilds
- **Stage 3 — Full Project (~15 min)** — generate frontend, backend, database, and APIs with multi-page navigation and business workflows
- **Edit before you build** — make corrections at every stage instead of waiting for a finished generation
- **Keep your Draft edits** — changes from the Draft stage are automatically preserved through full generation
- **Gain early visibility and control** — get faster feedback and transparency throughout the build

---

## Why Progressive Build matters

Unlike traditional AI builders that require you to wait for a complete generation before making corrections, Progressive Build gives you early visibility into what's being created. You can review, edit, and validate your project at every step — from the first editable draft to the fully generated application.

The result is faster feedback and greater control throughout the software creation process: catch issues while they're cheap to fix, shape the UI and business logic before the heavy generation runs, and avoid costly rebuilds.
