## What is AutoCoder.cc?

**AutoCoder.cc** is an AI-driven application and website generation tool. No programming or technical background is required. You simply describe your ideas in plain language, and AutoCoder.cc will transform them into functional applications, supporting web or mobile usage. You can download the source code for redeployment or freely modify it according to your needs.

---

## Who is AutoCoder for?

AutoCoder is designed to empower a wide range of creators:

- 🚀 **Startup founders** quickly building Minimum Viable Products (MVPs).
- 📊 **Product managers** turning Product Requirement Documents (PRDs) into usable prototypes.
- 🎨 **Designers** testing interactive experiences.
- 🧑‍💼 **Non-technical founders** launching their first product.
- 💻 **Indie developers** building internal tools or side projects.
- 🎓 **Students and job seekers** creating interactive resumes or personal portfolios.
- 💡 **Anyone** with creative ideas looking to bring them to life quickly.

---

## What’s the Core Value?

With AutoCoder, even if you have never written code, you can build applications faster and more cost-effectively. It is perfect for creative experiments, prototyping, or launching your first product.
