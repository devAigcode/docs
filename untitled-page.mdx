**SparkVibe** helps you discover outstanding projects in the AutoCoder.cc community, share your own work, and quickly create new projects through **Remix**. Through community showcases and project interactions, you can gain inspiration for software building, understand different project logics, and make product construction faster and better.

In the SparkVibe module, you can **browse** community projects, **like** and **Remix** projects, or publish your own projects to the community, allowing more users to browse and use them upon approval.

---

## Preview

You can view projects created by other users in the community and directly experience the Demo through the `Preview` feature.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-10.png)
</Frame>

In Preview mode, you can:

- View project page effects
- Switch between different pages
- Understand the overall structure of the project

---

## Remix

If you see a project whose structure or design fits your needs, you can directly **Remix** this project. 

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-11.png)
</Frame>

Remix will copy the project's structure and design, create a new project in your **Workspace**, and allow you to continue modifying and expanding this project. This way, you can quickly start new creations based on existing projects without having to build from scratch.

---

## Like

When you see a project you like, you can also click **Like** to save it.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-12.png)
</Frame>

Click Like to save a project. After liking, the project will be recorded in your **Liked Templates**, making it convenient for you to quickly view or reference it later.

---

## Search & Sorting

To help you quickly find projects of interest, SparkVibe provides search and sorting features.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-13.png)
</Frame>

Sorting

You can sort projects in the following ways to quickly discover trending applications in the community:

- **Popular**: Most popular projects
- **Latest**: Most recently published projects
- **Likes**: Projects with the most likes
- **Remix**: Most remixed projects

### Keyword Search

You can use the keyword search box to quickly find project content you are interested in. By entering keywords related to project names, industry types, page formats, or features, you can locate target projects more efficiently.

You can also combine category tags and sorting methods to further narrow down the filtering scope and improve browsing and searching efficiency.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-14.png)
</Frame>

**Examples:**

- When you want to find food and beverage projects, you can enter `Restaurant`.
- When you want to view landing page examples, you can enter `Landing Page`.
- If you are looking for mobile app projects, you can enter `Mobile App`.
