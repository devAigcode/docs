## General & onboarding

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is AutoCoder.cc suitable for users with no coding experience?">
    Yes. AutoCoder.cc is designed for users with no programming or technical background. You simply describe your ideas using natural language, and the platform automatically generates full-stack applications for you, including frontend UI, backend logic, and databases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which devices are supported?">
    AutoCoder.cc is a web-based cloud tool, requiring no installation. For the best building experience, we recommend using a desktop browser on Windows, macOS, or Linux. You can also use browsers on tablets or smartphones for lightweight access and quick modifications.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I input prompts in non-English languages?">
    Absolutely. AutoCoder.cc supports multi-language input. You can describe your project requirements or chat with the AI in your preferred language, and the system will accurately understand and respond accordingly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What should I do if the generation gets stuck or takes too long?">
    This may be due to network fluctuations or AI computational peaks. Try clicking **Refresh** or **Regenerate**. If the issue persists, contact our support team at [support@autocoder.cc](mailto:support@autocoder.cc) with an error screenshot and your registered email address.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Building & editing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if the initially generated page doesn't meet my expectations?">
    You can modify it in two ways:
    1. **Chat modification**: Use natural language to tell the AI to add pages or change structures.
    2. **Visual Editing**: Click the **Edit** button to enter edit mode, where you can directly click on the page to modify text, replace images, or change colors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I undo a mistake or revert to a previous version?">
    Yes. AutoCoder.cc provides a version rollback feature. Every time you save, it is recorded in the **Version History**. You can revert your project to any previous historical version at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I re-import exported code back into AutoCoder.cc after modifying it locally?">
    Currently, no. The platform only supports one-way export (downloading cloud code to your local machine). We do not support importing manually modified local code back into the platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Deployment & hosting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I publish projects on the Free plan?">
    Yes. All users, including those on the Free plan, are automatically provided with an `*.autocoder.cc` **Subdomain** for public access when publishing a project.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I bind my project to a Custom Domain?">
    After upgrading to the **Standard** or **Pro** plan, select **Custom Domain** in the **Publish** pop-up, enter your domain, and add a CNAME record pointing to AutoCoder.cc via your third-party domain provider. Verify the DNS and upload your SSL certificate to complete the binding.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will I lose my project data if I click Unpublish?">
    No. **Unpublish** only interrupts external public access to the project. Your project drafts and data remain safely stored in the **In Progress** tab of your **Workspace**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I directly publish the generated app to the App Store or Google Play?">
    Currently, no. The platform does not support direct pushing to app stores. We only support generating and downloading Android APK test packages for Android installation and testing. You must handle iOS test packages (.ipa) and official app store compliance and submission processes yourself.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing, credits & refunds

<Warning>
  If you have any questions regarding this section, please contact **[support@autocoder.cc](mailto:support@autocoder.cc)**!
</Warning>

**Plan comparison**

* **Free ($0/month)**: Ideal for experiencing vibe coding. Includes 10 credits (approx. 1 small project), unlimited initial subdomains, and community support.
* **Standard ($25/month)**: Designed for creators and personal brands. Includes 45 credits (approx. 3-5 complete projects), **Source Code Export**, unlimited initial subdomains, and email support.
* **Pro ($60/month - Most popular)**: Built for heavy users and startup teams. Includes 180 credits (approx. 8-12 large projects), priority access to new AI models, **Source Code Export**, unlimited initial subdomains, and priority email support.

<Note>
  **Tip**: You can earn extra free credits by completing missions in the top right corner.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do unused plan credits roll over to the next month?">
    No. Plan credits are valid for one calendar month from the date of purchase. To encourage continuous creation, unused credits will be cleared after the plan expires. To continue using credits, please purchase the corresponding plan package again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How are my previous credits handled if I purchase or upgrade to a new plan?">
    After upgrading, the system prioritizes the credits from your new plan. If the new plan's credits are exhausted, and your previous plan's credits are still within their 30-day validity period, you can continue to use the remaining old credits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you support refunds? What if the generation fails?">
    The platform enforces the following refund and compensation policies:
    1. **Full/Partial refunds**: You can apply for a refund (subject to processing fees) only if you purchased directly via AutoCoder.cc, request it within **7 days** of purchase, and **have not consumed any services or credits**. Consumed services are not eligible for fiat refunds.
    2. **Credit compensation**: If a generation fails completely due to system bugs or errors, we generally do not issue fiat refunds. However, you can contact `support@autocoder.cc` with error screenshots and usage records. Upon verification, we will fully refund the credits consumed by the error.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel auto-renewal?">
    The platform currently operates on a "one-time payment (monthly quota)" model. There is no auto-renewal mechanism, and no unauthorized recurring charges will occur. Therefore, you do not need to manually cancel a subscription.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Source code export & ownership

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is included in the exported source code? What is the quality?">
    The export provides a complete full-stack code package (including frontend, backend, and database schemas) and supports both Web and App projects. The code is well-structured and includes the necessary environment configurations. Simply follow the included `README.md` instructions and run `npm install` to get your project running locally in minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I sync my project directly to GitHub?">
    Currently, projects are exported as a ZIP file, which you need to manually push to your GitHub repository. Native GitHub one-click synchronization is actively under development, so stay tuned.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I retain 100% copyright? Are there limits on export times?">
    Yes, you own 100% of the generated code and can freely use it for commercial purposes or secondary development. **Source Code Export** is an exclusive feature for **Standard** and **Pro** plans, with no limits on the number of exports during your active validity period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Data privacy & security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I recover my data after deleting my account?">
    No. We respect your privacy, and you can choose to **Delete account** in your account settings. However, please note that account deletion takes effect immediately and is irreversible. All your generated projects, remaining credits, and underlying data will be permanently cleared.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What should I do if I forget my password or cannot log in?">
    Please follow these troubleshooting steps:
    1. Ensure your email is spelled correctly and Caps Lock is off.
    2. Ensure your network is stable, and try clearing your browser cache or switching to a different browser.
    3. If you still cannot log in, send your registered email address and a screenshot of the error interface to [support@autocoder.cc](mailto:support@autocoder.cc), and we will assist you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does the platform manage my data?">
    AutoCoder.cc strictly complies with relevant data privacy regulations. Your data is securely protected by our [Privacy Policy](/security/privacy) and [Terms of Service](/security/terms). 
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
