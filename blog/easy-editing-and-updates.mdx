## What you'll learn

- **Turn your idea into a clear Requirement List** — your app's blueprint before generation starts
- **Generate a working demo across web, mobile, and backend views** — preview every surface from one prompt
- **Update your app using simple chat instructions** — change content and structure in plain language
- **Edit content and design visually with click-and-edit controls** — fine-tune without leaving the page
- **Track changes and manage versions with built-in history** — roll back or branch at any time
