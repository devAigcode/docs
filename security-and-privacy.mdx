## Account authentication

**Account authentication** ensures that only authorized users can access and manage their projects and data.

### Login & registration

Register and log in to your AutoCoder.cc account to access full platform features, such as:

- Create projects
- Edit demos
- **Remix** community projects
- Manage projects in your **Workspace**

After logging in, your projects, mission progress, and account information are automatically saved to your account.

### Google One Tap login

To simplify the login process, AutoCoder.cc supports **Google One Tap** login. When logging in with Google, you can:

- Click **Sign in with Google**
- Select your Google account
- Complete the login

This method allows you to quickly access AutoCoder.cc and build applications without an additional password.
