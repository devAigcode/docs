## Upgrade Membership

Click "**Upgrade**" in the Personal Center or "Pricing" in the top navigation bar of the homepage to view the complete pricing plans and compare benefits. By subscribing to a paid plan, you unlock premium features such as source code export, Custom Domain, the latest models, and priority access to new features.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-34.png)
</Frame>

**Plan Recommendation**: We strongly recommend prioritizing the **PRO** plan to experience the platform's full capabilities.

<Note>
  **Please note**: Membership credit benefits are only valid during the active membership period. Once the membership expires, any unused membership credits will be cleared to zero.
</Note>

## Account Management

Click the "**Account**" option to maintain your personal identity information and account security.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-29.png)
</Frame>

- **Edit Profile**: You can modify your Username here. Please note that to ensure account security, the Email address bound during registration currently cannot be modified directly within the app.
- **Change Password**: Regularly update your password to protect your account security. Changing the password requires email verification; once verified, you can modify it.
- **Delete Account**: If you no longer use AutoCoder.cc, you can choose to delete your account.

<Warning>
  **Warning**: The account deletion **takes effect immediately** and is **irreversible**. Once confirmed, all your data (including generated projects, remaining credits, and account history) will be permanently cleared and cannot be recovered. If you currently have an active paid subscription, it will also be canceled immediately. **Please proceed with caution.**
</Warning>

## Billing Management

Click "**Manage Billing**" to securely redirect to the official Stripe billing portal. Here, you can independently complete the following operations:

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-32.png)
</Frame>

- **Manage Payment Methods**: Add, remove, or update your payment channels. Currently supported methods include Stripe Link fast checkout, as well as major credit cards like Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and UnionPay.
- **Update Billing Information**: Modify the email address used to receive electronic invoices and billing notifications.
- **View Billing History**: Check historical deduction details and download electronic invoices.

## Feedback

If you encounter any system bugs, functional anomalies, or have suggestions for improvement while using the platform, please click "**Feedback**":

- In the built-in system form that pops up, select the corresponding "**Feedback Type**", describe your issue in detail (up to 1000 characters), and click "**Submit**" to send it directly to the product and research team.
- For more in-depth customer support or other business collaboration inquiries, you can also email the official support team directly at: `support@autocoder.cc`.
