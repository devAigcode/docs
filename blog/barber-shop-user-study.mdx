## 1. User Background

**Name:** Arthur Shelby<br/>
**Age:** 34<br/>
**Location:** Los Angeles, California, USA<br/>
**Shop:** Arthur's Barber Shop, a small barbershop with 3 barbers, serving around 25 customers per day<br/>
**Goals:** Increase online visibility, enable online booking for customers, and showcase the shop's style and services<br/>
**Technical Skills:** Familiar with basic computer operations and social media, but no website development experience<br/>

**Psychological and Behavioral Traits:**
- Values customer experience and wants clients to view services, prices, and barber profiles online
- Aims to reduce phone communication and improve appointment efficiency
- Prefers a simple, intuitive interface for quick onboarding

---

## 2. User Needs

### Pain Points:
- **High Technical Barrier:** Most barbershop owners are reluctant to learn website development; there is strong demand for quick website generation.
- **Visual and Simple Operation:** Templates and visual editors are key to improving user experience.
- **Online Booking is Most Popular:** Reduces phone workload, prevents scheduling conflicts, and enhances customer satisfaction.
- **Full Ownership Increases Trust:** Shop owners want the website to belong to them rather than relying on third-party platforms.
- **Marketing Potential:** With an AutoCoder.cc website, barbershops can integrate social media promotion to drive customer growth.

### Online Display Needs:
- Showcase shop environment, barber team, services, and pricing
- Mobile, tablet, and desktop access
- Rich visual content reflecting brand style

### Online Booking System:
- Customers can select services, time, and barber
- Automatic appointment confirmation and reminders

### Customer Management:
- Record customer information and appointment history
- Support memberships and repeat-visit reminders

### Self-Management and Brand Control:
- Ability to modify page content anytime (e.g., price updates, promotions)
- Option to connect a custom domain and export the code
