## Commission Policy

In addition to Credits rewards, qualified referrals may generate commission.

Commission is calculated based on 30% of the invited user's first eligible payment only, before taxes and after any discounts, coupons, credits, or promotions are applied.

This commission applies to the invited user's first paid purchase only. Renewals, recurring subscriptions, and subsequent purchases are not eligible for additional commission.

---

## Payment Process

To ensure fair payouts and prevent abuse:

- Commission becomes eligible after a 30-day review period.
- The referred user must have no refund, dispute, chargeback, or abnormal refund activity during that period.
- Commission payouts are processed via PayPal within 7 business days after the review period.
- Users are responsible for providing a valid PayPal account.
- If a valid PayPal account is not provided within 30 days of commission approval, the commission may be forfeited.
- All PayPal fees, transfer fees, and transaction fees are the responsibility of the recipient.

---

## Program Rules

To keep the program fair for everyone:

- Rewards apply only to new users who register through your referral link.
- Referred users must complete registration within 14 days of clicking your referral link.
- Each reward is granted only once per referred user.
- All earned Credits are valid for 6 months from the earning date.
- Rewards are not available for duplicate accounts, self-referrals, or suspicious activity.
- We do not grant rewards to users registered with disposable, temporary, or high-risk email addresses.

---

## Fraud and Abuse

AutoCoder reserves the right to review, withhold, cancel, revoke, or recover rewards and commission in cases including but not limited to:

- Fake accounts
- Self-referrals
- Fraudulent payments or abnormal refunds
- Abnormal referral activity
- Abuse of the referral system or violations of platform policies

Accounts suspected of abuse may be suspended or permanently removed from the Referral Program without prior notice.
