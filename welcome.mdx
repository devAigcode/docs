## What is AutoCoder.cc?

**AutoCoder.cc** is an AI-driven application and website generation tool that requires no programming or technical background. Simply describe your ideas in plain language, and AutoCoder.cc will transform them into functional web or mobile applications. You can export the source code for redeployment or modify it to suit your needs.

---

## Who is AutoCoder.cc for?

AutoCoder.cc is designed to empower a wide range of creators:

- 🚀 **Startup founders** quickly building Minimum Viable Products (MVPs).
- 📊 **Product managers** turning Product Requirement Documents (PRDs) into usable prototypes.
- 🎨 **Designers** testing interactive experiences.
- 🧑‍💼 **Non-technical founders** launching their first product.
- 💻 **Indie developers** building internal tools or side projects.
- 🎓 **Students and job seekers** creating interactive resumes or personal portfolios.
- 💡 **Anyone** with creative ideas looking to bring them to life quickly.
